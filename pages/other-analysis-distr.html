Score Distributions

What is the distribution of all barbershop quartet scores?

What is the average quartet score? At any given contest, the average score will vary depending on the strength of the region, and individual quartet performances. How does one even measure a region's "strength"? This makes it difficult to understand how an individual performance fares when compared to the other performances worldwide.

Below is a chart of quartet scores going back 10 years, with filters for Org, Region, and Group (BHS International / Other).

The most notable observation overall is that the distribution is almost bimodal - it has a peak in the high 60s, and then again between 72 and 74. While volume drops when filtering, we do see similar bumps when looking at many of the organizations in isolation so it doesn't seem to be specific to BHS (Which makes up the largest portion of the data)

Initial hypothesis was that the inclusion of contests like BHS International, or the European Championship might contribute to this effect, but excluding these has little effect below 75.

The reason for the bimodal distribution is not yet clear and I plan on analysing further at a future date
