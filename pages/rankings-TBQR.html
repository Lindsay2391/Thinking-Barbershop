TBQR

THINKING BARBERSHOP QUARTET RANKINGS

An international ranking of quartets across BHS and Alliance Organizations

BIQR is designed to rank quartets from all across the world, outside of just the BHS international contest. This system ranks quartets on a weighted average of scores, reflecting each quartet's performance over the past 12 months.



The purpose behind TBQR is to recognize the talented barbershop quartets across the world, acknowledging that there can be a multitude of reasons why a group may not be able to attend BHS International.


It also recognizes that an individual performance can be impacted by factors outside a quartet's control so while the ranking places a high importance on the most recent result, it also factors in other performances over a 12 month span.



The system calculates the rankings based on a weighted average of scores that quartets receive from competitions they participate in during the last 12 months.


The scores are from publically available scoresheets published by BHS and alliance organizations (BHA, BHNZ, BING!, BABS, BIBA, HH, IABS, LABBS, SNOBS)



Each score a quartet receives is weighted by its relative timing and importance. The weighting for each score is determined by dividing the score by the rank of the contest by date, with the most recent contest given the highest priority.
BHS International, Midwinter, and Varsity contests are given a full weighting of 1, reflecting its importance in the ranking calculation.
