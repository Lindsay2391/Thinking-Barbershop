About Thinking Barbershop

Thinking Barbershop is a project close to my heart. I’m bringing together scores from different barbershop organizations all in one place. The goal is to make these resources easily accessible to everyone in the barbershop community.

Whether you’re a singer, arranger, or just a fan, I hope Thinking Barbershop has something for you. With a collection of scores, analyses, and search tools, it’s designed to help you explore and enjoy the world of barbershop harmony.
