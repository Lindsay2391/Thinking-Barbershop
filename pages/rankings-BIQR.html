BIQR is designed to rank quartets from barbershop quartet competitions across BHS and Alliance Organizations. This system ranks quartets on a weighted average of scores, reflecting each quartet's performance over the past 12 months.

- The system calculates the rankings based on a weighted average of scores that quartets receive from competitions they participate in during the last 12 months.
- The scores are from publically available scoresheets published by BHS and alliance organizations (BHA, BHNZ, BING!, BABS, BIBA, HH, IABS, LABBS, SNOBS)

- Each score a quartet receives is weighted by its relative timing and importance.
- The weighting for each score is determined by dividing the score by the rank of the contest by date, with the most recent contest given the highest priority.
- BHS International, Midwinter, and Varsity contests are given a full weighting of 1, reflecting its importance in the ranking calculation.

Updating Rankings:

- Rankings are dynamically updated to reflect the latest competitions and scores, ensuring an up-to-date reflection of each quartet's standing in the international barbershop quartet community.
