Frequently Asked Questions

What organizations are included in the score database?

The database currently includes scores from BABS, BHA, BHNZ, BHS, BIBA, BING, IABS, LABBS, and SNOBS. Scoresheet coverage and availability varies between organizations.

Visit Our Data for more details

How often are the scores updated?

Scores for new contests come through as soon as possible once the scoresheet PDFs are available. There will also be additional updates as more historical data is added.

This is a personal project and as such there may be delays due to personal circumstances.
