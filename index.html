FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What organizations are included in the score database?

The database currently includes scores from BABS, BHA, BHNZ, BHS, BIBA, BING, IABS, LABBS, and SNOBS. Scoresheet coverage and availability varies between organizations.

Visit Our Data for more details.

How often are the scores updated?

Scores for new contests will come through once the scoresheet PDFs are available. There will also be additional updates as more historical data is added.

If the scores aren't there yet don't worry. This is a personal project and as such there may be delays but I will update with the latest results as soon as possible.

How accurate is the data?

While updating the scores does not require any manual input, meaning the scores will have a low error rate, there has been some work standardizing song names with different spelling, quartet names across organizations, and contest names which may not be perfect.

I recommend checkout out Our Data for more details, and don't hesitate to get in touch via email or facebook if you notice any discrepencies.

Will Sweet Adelines and Harmony Inc be added?

Eventually yes!

Each organization brings its own nuances and challenges, however once BHS and Alliance quartet data is more comprehensive, and choruses scores are added, the next step will be SAI & HI.

Keep an eye on the Dev Blog for future updates!
